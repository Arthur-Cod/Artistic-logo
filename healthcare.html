What we offer:

Navigating a healthcare website shouldn’t feel like you’re trying to decipher a medical encyclopedia. We focus on creating user-friendly designs that everyone can navigate, including those with disabilities. It’s all about making sure patients can find what they need without a hitch—whether it’s booking an appointment or understanding complex medical information. Accessibility isn’t just a box-ticking exercise for us; it’s a fundamental part of how we approach every project.

Why choose KOTA?

Accessibility features for all patients

From elderly users to those with disabilities, our sites ensure everyone can navigate smoothly and securely, accessing the information they need without barriers.

Design-driven operational efficiencies

Boosting patient engagement
