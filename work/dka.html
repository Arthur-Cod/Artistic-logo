Having recently undergone a complete company rebrand, DK&A now wanted both websites to match the style and feel of their new cohesive look. It was important for us to define a stronger connection between the two brands, the two websites needed to feel distinct but also connected.

DK&A

Our main objective for the DK&A agency brief was to build an innovative and engaging website that clearly communicates their work and the services DK&A offer. In order to attract new clients and gain positive feedback from associates, the website needed to be easy and intuitive for the client to keep up-to-date with all their latest case studies, content and visuals.

As DK&A are a design company, we wanted to make sure the new websites to felt creative and exciting for users to navigate. Part of their new branding are the acid ampersand and molecules, throughout the website we animated these elements bringing them to life as a point of difference in the industry.

As a result we have designed and built an industry-leading website showcasing DK&A‚Äôs services, culture and thought leadership in an exciting, creative and engaging way.

Design Thinkers Academy

The Design Thinkers Academy London is the coaching and leadership academy of DK&A, they organise live and online courses, events, lectures and personal coaching programs for businesses looking to innovate & change the way you work.

Although the main success factor for this website was to streamline DTA’s E-Commerce platform and therefore increase course sales, it also needed to be regarded as a place of learning where users can find informative resources, blog and podcasts.

As a result of a collaborative design and development process, we have built a beautiful website that balances DTA’s creative brand identity with an intuitive Shopify platform where they can sell all their courses.
