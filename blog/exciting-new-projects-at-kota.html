Exciting new projects at KOTA!

April 1st 2021

By Jonny

Meet our new friends!

We’re very proud of the partnerships we’ve formed recently. All of our new clients have an appreciation for quality design and, with our help, want to innovate within their respective industries. Find out more about the 10+ projects we’re working on right now!

 

Sonovate: Finance technology provider.

Managing a large freelance workforce can be a hassle and Sonovate are on a mission to get people paid easily and on time. They have created an innovative end-to-end invoice financing solution empowering all kinds of businesses – from start-ups to large enterprises.

Sonovate partnered with us to create a new, intuitive website which will give their marketing team lots of flexibility to tailor it to the company needs – present and future. We want to improve their conversion rate by streamlining the user experience, as well as help Sonovate work towards better rankings in organic search engines through technical SEO.

www.sonovate.com

 

Little’s: Flavoured instant coffee company.

For over 25 years, Little’s have been proving instant coffee can be delicious and fun, with their flavoured instant coffee range. Their products contain no sugar and no nasties, are low cal and vegan, packaged in an eco-friendly way, what else could you ask for?

You can find their products in Sainsbury’s, Waitrose and Whole Foods or, alternatively, shop online. That’s where KOTA comes in, as we’ll be working on transforming their E-commerce platform to give their customers a superb shopping experience to foster loyalty while allowing Little’s to hit their sales targets.

www.wearelittles.com

 

tangerine: International innovation and design consultancy.

tangerine solve some of the world’s biggest design challenges in transportation, retail and hospitality, as well as product and UX. From their 3 studios in UK, South Korea and Brazil, they work on international projects for the largest global corporations such as Unilever, Samsung, Toyota and British Airways.

We’re honoured to have been chosen by tangerine to deliver a new website which will be aligned with the quality of design work they produce for their clients.

www.tangerine.net

 

TEMES: The leading tourism and real estate investor in Greece.

Since 1997, TEMES have been continuously growing their portfolio to become the leading high-end tourism and real estate investor, developer and operator in Greece. The group own Costa Navarino, one of the largest tourism investments in the Mediterranean, and partner with a number of other Greek luxury hotel developers.

For this project, KOTA are excited to utilise our experience in both the hospitality and luxury sectors. Our team is working with TEMES to create a website which will position them as the leader in high-end tourism and introduce users to the inspiring history of their exceptional growth.

www.costanavarino.com

 

HollandGreen: Architecture, interior design and landscape design practice.

We have been working with HollandGreen for many years now and while the current website
has won many awards and still looks amazing, the business has expanded into new service areas and so HG need a new way to present them. Thanks to our long-term partnership, KOTA were a natural choice to deliver a new platform to host their three practices. See the case study for the current website here.

www.hollandgreen.co.uk

 

Harrison Eidsgaard: Leading yacht design studio.

After launching the new website for SuperYachtsMonaco in December 2020, we were intrigued to see if this would lead to further work in the yachting industry. What a great start to the year it was when Harrison Eidsgaard, one of the world’s leading yacht designers, appointed KOTA to digitally transform their online offering. The glowing testimony provided by the SuperYachtsMonaco team definitely helped us win this project!

www.eidsgaard.com

 

Poplar: AR platform and design studio.

Augmented Reality is already big, but we believe it’s going to be even bigger over the next few years! That’s why we’re excited to partner with Poplar Studio who are a creative platform that provides AR and 3D experiences on-demand, including AR marketing (think viral TikTok trends) and AR commerce experiences.

Together, we’re working on the digital evolution of the Poplar brand, which will lead to the delivery of a new website design. Their marketing team were especially attracted to the conceptual approach we specialise in, as they look to stand out in this innovative sector.

www.poplar.studio

 

LoveBrands: Fashion agents and distributors.

For over a decade now, LoveBrands have been partnering with internationally recognised designers such as Joop and Guess to distribute their latest collections. LoveBrands have approached us to craft an exciting brand that will stand the test of time.

We have also been commissioned to deliver a new website that can inspire users by showcasing LovaBrands’ editorial photography and videography.

www.lovebrandsuk.com

 

Crux: Product design studio.

Based in Bristol, Crux develop intelligent and beautiful products that excel in the real world. With clients such as Unilever, Hewlett Packard, GSK and Gillette, Crux are an internationally respected design studio and we are very excited to bring the business to life online. Our idea centres around a WebGL concept to communicate their process in an engaging way.

www.cruxproductdesign.com

 

Tribepad: Recruitment software company.

TribePad’s award-winning recruitment software is used by more than 20 million people, in 15 languages worldwide. Their clients include Tesco, KFC and BBC. After a competitive pitch, TribePad had this to say about us:

Throughout the process you guys have been responsive, helpful and well organised (well that’s how it seemed!). Your proposal and examples were the best designs we saw, you seemed to address our points from the brief, and we’re excited about the possibilities of working with you. We really want to raise our game and we think you will push us to have the best site we can.

During the process, we met with one of the founders of TribePad. It’s clear they are a hugely innovative and exciting company but with a great focus on the people. Lockdown boosted their business performance so much that they wanted to give back to the community in Sheffield by announcing a £1 million fund to support local tech startups.

www.tribepad.com

 

Shawarmer: Saudi Arabia’s largest restaurant group.

Inspired by our work across brand and digital in Europe, Shawarmer have commissioned KOTA to create a bilingual website in English and Arabic to support the International plans of the group. With over 100 sites in Saudi Arabia, Shawarmer have big plans to open more restaurants across the globe. Hungry yet?

www.shawarmer.com
